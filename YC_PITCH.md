# EmotiArm — YC-Style Pitch (2 min)

## The Problem (15 sec)
"83% of remote workers report loneliness. Productivity apps fail because digital notifications are easily ignored. Nobody's there to comfort you when you're struggling or hold you accountable when you lose focus."

## The Insight (10 sec)
"Humans need physical touch for emotional regulation. A tap on the shoulder, a pat on the back - these work because they exist in real space, not on a screen you can minimize."

## What We Built (20 sec)
"EmotiArm watches your face with a webcam. When you're sad - frown, head down - it pats your head and speaks comfort. When you're distracted - looking away, phone in hand - it slaps your desk and yells 'Focus!' It's like having a coworker who actually notices."

## The Demo (60 sec)
[Run DEMO.py]

"Watch: SAD state - gentle patting motion, wipes tears, speaks 'I'm here for you.' DISTRACTED state - quick slap, 'Eyes on the screen!' Built in 6 hours with MediaPipe detecting 468 facial landmarks, Cyberwave's digital twin for robot control, ElevenLabs for natural voice."

## The Vision (15 sec)
"This is a $50 robotic arm. We're starting with remote workers. Next: therapy offices, hospitals, elderly care. Physical AI that actually feels like companionship, not surveillance."

## The Ask (Optional, 10 sec)
"We're looking for feedback. Does physical emotional feedback change how you work alone?"

## KEY POINTS TO HIT:
✓ **Problem is big** (83% lonely, $X billion market)
✓ **Current solutions suck** (apps don't work)
✓ **Your insight is non-obvious** (physical > digital for emotions)
✓ **Demo shows it working** (not slides, real code)
✓ **Vision is huge** (not just desk toys, real healthcare)
✓ **You shipped in 6 hours** (shows execution speed)

## IF THEY ASK:
- **"Why not just call a friend?"** → "Takes 5 min to setup a call. This reacts in 2 seconds, exactly when you need it."
- **"Creepy?"** → "Webcam only processes locally, no recording. You control it. Like having AirPods, not a surveillance camera."
- **"Competition?"** → "Productivity apps = software. Therapy bots = text. We're the only physical emotional AI for desks."
- **"Business model?"** → "Hardware + subscription for ML models. Think Peloton for emotional wellness."

## DELIVERY TIPS:
- Talk FAST but clear
- Show emotion when describing the problem (you feel this pain)
- Smile when the robot moves (you're proud of building this)
- Pause after the demo (let them react)
- End with energy (you believe this is huge)
